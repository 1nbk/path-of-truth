About Our Ministry

Path Of Truth Ministries is a Christ-centered community established to guide believers on their spiritual journey. We are a fellowship dedicated to studying God's Word, growing in faith, and reflecting the love of Christ in every aspect of life.

Founded on the unchanging truth of Scripture, we believe that Jesus Christ is the Way, the Truth, and the Life. Our ministry exists to help believers walk faithfully on this path, equipped with biblical knowledge, spiritual maturity, and a heart for service.

We are more than a church; we are a family united by faith, strengthened by prayer, and committed to raising ambassadors for Christ who will impact their communities, workplaces, and nations with the Gospel.

Our Mission

To build a community of believers who are rooted in God's Word, equipped for ministry, and passionate about living out the Great Commission. We exist to make disciples who make disciples.

Our Vision

To see a generation of Spirit-filled believers who walk in truth, demonstrate Christ's love, and transform their world through the power of the Gospel.

Our Core Values

Biblical Truth

We stand firmly on the authority and sufficiency of Scripture as our foundation for faith and practice.

Prayer & Worship

We prioritize intimacy with God through fervent prayer and heartfelt worship in spirit and truth.

Community & Fellowship

We believe in authentic relationships where believers encourage, support, and grow together in love.

Discipleship

We are committed to intentional spiritual growth and mentoring believers to maturity in Christ.

Kingdom Impact

We equip believers to be ambassadors for Christ, representing Him in every sphere of influence.

Love & Grace

We extend God's unconditional love and grace to all, creating a welcoming environment for everyone.
